Zero Waste Living Tips

Published on March 15, 2024

Zero waste living is about reducing our environmental impact by minimizing the waste we produce. Here's a comprehensive guide to help you get started on your zero waste journey.

The 5 R's of Zero Waste

  1. Refuse what you don't need
  2. Reduce what you do need
  3. Reuse what you consume
  4. Recycle what you can't refuse, reduce, or reuse
  5. Rot (compost) the rest

Quick Start Tip

Begin your zero waste journey by conducting a waste audit. Track your trash for a week to identify your biggest sources of waste.

Simple Swaps for Zero Waste Living

  • Replace plastic bags with reusable shopping bags
  • Use a reusable water bottle instead of disposable ones
  • Switch to cloth napkins instead of paper
  • Choose package-free products when possible
  • Use beeswax wraps instead of plastic wrap

Zero Waste Shopping Tips

  • Bring your own containers and bags
  • Shop at bulk stores when possible
  • Choose products with minimal or recyclable packaging
  • Buy fresh produce without packaging
  • Support local farmers markets

Zero Waste in the Kitchen

The kitchen is often the biggest source of household waste. Here are some tips to reduce kitchen waste:

  • Plan meals to reduce food waste
  • Store food properly to extend its life
  • Use reusable containers for leftovers
  • Compost food scraps
  • Make your own cleaning solutions
